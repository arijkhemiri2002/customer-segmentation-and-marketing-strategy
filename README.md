Customer Segmentation Project:

This project applies K-Means clustering to segment customers based on their purchasing behavior.

**Model Results
Model:Kmeans
Number of clusters: 2
Silhouette Score: 0.3697
Total customers: 2213

**Customer Segments
 *Cluster 0 – Price-Sensitive Customers
Low income
Low spending
Low purchase frequency
Higher number of children

=>These customers have limited budgets and are less active buyers.

 *Cluster 1 – High-Value Customers (VIP)
High income
High spending
Frequent purchases
Fewer children

=> These customers are the most profitable and highly engaged.

**Business Recommendations
Cluster 0
Discounts and promotions
Family offers
Price-focused marketing
Cluster 1
Loyalty programs
Premium offers
Personalized recommendations

**Conclusion
The clustering reveals two distinct customer profiles:
 *Budget-constrained customers
 *High-value customers

=>This segmentation can help businesses optimize marketing strategies and increase revenue.
